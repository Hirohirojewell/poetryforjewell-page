For the Girl I Admire

In her eyes, the stars align,

A subtle spark, a perfect sign.

Her laugh—a melody, soft and sweet,

Like summer rain, cool and complete.

Her presence, a light that fills the air,

With every glance, I find wonder there.

She moves with grace, like art in motion,

Stirring dreams, like waves in the ocean.

A heart so kind, with words that heal,

In her, the world feels more real.

I stand in awe, though words fall shy,

For she is the moon lighting my sky.

— A quiet admirer
